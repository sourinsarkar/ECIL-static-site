How the distribution channel works?

A distribution substation is located near or inside city/town/village/ industrial area. It receives power from a transmission network. The high voltage from the transmission line is then stepped down by a step-down transformer to the primary distribution level voltage.

How can I register a complaint regarding connection issues?

Registered users can login to lodge a complaint regarding long pending electricity issues with the Electricity Corp. of India Limited. One can register a complaint pertaining to electricity supply interruptions, breakdown, service connection issues etc.
